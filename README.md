### 👋 About

I work at the intersection of **LLM agents**, **self-improving / self-evolving systems**, and **agent safety (misevolution)**.

I like turning fast-moving papers into things you can actually **read**, **run**, and **reason about** — curated lists, small runnable demos, and honest notes on how these systems fail.
